Layers

Layer 4: Transport layer

  • Some tasks performed at this layer:
    • Process-Level Addressing (ports)
    • Mux/de-mux
    • Segmentation, packaging and reassembly (this is similar to, but not the same, as Layer 3 fragmentation)
    • Acknowledgments and Retransmission
    • Flow control
  • This is where reliability is defined (in TCP/IP)
